Cougar

The cougar is more closely related to smaller felines, including the domestic cat than to any subspecies of lion.

  • Average length: 2.2 m
  • Tail length: 63-95 cm
  • Weight: 53-100 k
  • Top running speed: 64-80 kph
  • Able to jumb as high as: 5.5 m

The cougar is an ambush predator and pursues a wide variety of prey. Primary food sources are ungulates, particularly deer, but also livestock. It also hunts species as small as insects and rodents. This cat prefers habitats with dense underbrush and rocky areas for stalking, but can also live in open areas.
